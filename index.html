Meet Dorifuto Mai


Welcome to the official page of Dorifuto Mai

At just 28 years old, Dorifuto Mai has become a rising star in the global EDM scene and a fearless competitor on the high-speed drifting circuits. Known for her boundary-pushing beats and intense performances, Dorifuto isn’t just a DJ—she’s a trendsetter, a pioneer, and a living testament to the power of following your passions, no matter how unconventional.



Where Music Meets Speed

Born and raised in Tokyo, Japan, Dorifuto Mai’s life has always been a thrilling blend of contrasts. From a young age, she was immersed in both music and speed—her father was a DJ, and her mother was an amateur drifter. With these influences, it’s no surprise that Mai developed a deep love for both electronic beats and the art of drifting. As a teenager, she began DJing and fell in love with the energy and raw power of electronic dance music, especially house music's captivating rhythms. Simultaneously, she was learning the art of drifting from her mother, tearing through Tokyo's streets in a Toyota AE86—a car that embodies precision and agility, much like her on stage.


The fusion of these two passions was inevitable. At 18, Dorifuto moved to Los Angeles to pursue her dream of becoming a DJ. While her peers were focused solely on music, she knew she wanted to master both music and speed, which would soon define her unique approach to performance.



A New Wave in EDM

Dorifuto’s breakthrough moment came in 2016 with the release of Midnight Drift, her debut EP that took the underground house scene by storm. Tracks like “Turbo Pulse” mixed gritty basslines with vibrant melodies and electrifying drops, making her a favorite on every dance floor. Her live shows quickly became legendary, known for their intensity and her ability to command the crowd with unmatched energy.

From the mainstages of Ultra Music Festival and Tomorrowland to intimate underground raves, Dorifuto’s music has been the heartbeat of countless unforgettable nights. But she’s more than just a DJ—she’s a creator, a visionary, and a storyteller. Every set she plays is like a high-speed chase, every drop a drift into new sonic territory.



The Drifting Phenomenon

In 2018, Dorifuto expanded her horizons and made her mark in motorsport, becoming the first female DJ to compete professionally in Formula Drift. Her signature style behind the wheel is a perfect mix of precision, control, and pure instinct. It's no surprise she earned the nickname "The Drift Queen." Watching her on the track is like witnessing a live remix, where every drift syncs perfectly with the beat of her own inner soundtrack.


In just a few short years, Dorifuto has secured her place among the world’s top drift drivers, earning respect from fellow competitors and fans alike. What sets her apart isn’t just her skill on the track—it’s her ability to seamlessly integrate her two worlds. At her performances, she often combines high-speed drifting stunts with her electrifying DJ sets, creating a multi-sensory experience that has audiences hooked from the first beat to the last drift.



Breaking Barriers, Shaping Culture

Dorifuto Mai is more than just a talented DJ; she’s a cultural icon in the making. As one of the few women in the male-dominated drifting scene, she continues to prove that no dream is too big to chase. Whether she’s on stage, behind the wheel, or pushing boundaries in new ways, Mai inspires fans around the globe to live louder, think bigger, and pursue their own passions with fearless determination.


Her success goes beyond what she’s accomplished—it’s about how she does it. Dorifuto blends the worlds of art, technology, and innovation to create a unique aesthetic that feels both fresh and timeless. Her fans know that Dorifuto isn’t just a moment—she’s shaping the future of music, motorsport, and culture.



The Road Ahead

What’s next for Dorifuto Mai? The question isn’t so much “What’s next?” as it is “What *isn't* next?” Her upcoming album Accelerate promises to take her sound to new heights, featuring collaborations with some of the biggest names in EDM. In motorsport, she’s eyeing the podium in Formula Drift, with even bigger ambitions on the horizon. Could rally racing be her next challenge?



Whatever she sets her sights on, one thing is certain: Dorifuto Mai is just getting started. Expect the unexpected, because this is only the beginning.

Car Girls

Car Girls: Leading the Way in Automotive Passion In the traditionally male-dominated world of automobiles, a new breed of enthusiasts is shifting gears and breaking stereotypes: the "Car Girls." These automotive aficionados are not just defying expectations; they are leading the way with their passion, knowledge, and love for all things automotive.

Car girls are women who share a profound love for cars, trucks, motorcycles, and everything in between. They are the ones who can tell you the horsepower of a Mustang GT, the torque of a Dodge Challenger, or the handling characteristics of a Porsche 911. They are not just passengers in the automotive world; they are the drivers, the mechanics, the engineers, the racers, and the collectors.

From Enthusiasts to Influencers

What sets car girls apart is not just their passion but also their influence. With the rise of social media, many car girls have taken to platforms like Instagram, YouTube, and TikTok to share their experiences, knowledge, and projects. They have amassed followings in the tens of thousands and even millions, becoming influential figures in the automotive community.

These influencers are not just posing with cars for likes; they are creating content that educates, entertains, and inspires. From DIY tutorials on basic car maintenance to in-depth reviews of the latest models, they are helping others navigate the world of automobiles with confidence.

Breaking Stereotypes

Car girls are shattering stereotypes that cars are only for men or that women aren't as knowledgeable about automotive topics. They are proving that anyone, regardless of gender, can be a gearhead. Their presence at car shows, races, and automotive events is a testament to their love for the industry and their determination to be a part of it.

Moreover, car girls are not limited to a specific age group. They come from all walks of life, from teenagers working on their first project cars to seasoned veterans who have been in the industry for decades. This diversity is a beautiful reflection of the inclusive nature of the automotive community.

Leading by Example

In addition to their online presence, car girls are making waves in the real world. They are competing in motorsport events, restoring classic cars, and working in various roles within the automotive industry. From automotive journalists to race car drivers, from engineers to mechanics, they are proving that women have a place at every level of the automotive world.

Their accomplishments serve as inspiration for the next generation of car enthusiasts, especially young girls who might have been told that cars are not for them. By showcasing their skills, knowledge, and achievements, car girls are paving the way for a more inclusive and diverse automotive future.

Empowering the Next Generationn of Car Girls

One of the most significant impacts of car girls is on the next generation. Through mentorship programs, workshops, and community events, they are empowering young girls to pursue their passion for cars. By providing guidance, support, and resources, they are creating a welcoming environment for future generations of car enthusiasts.

By being visible, vocal, and unapologetically passionate about their love for cars, car girls are changing perceptions and opening doors. They are proving that the automotive world is not limited by gender but rather defined by a shared love for all things on four wheels (or two).

Car girls are not just a trend; they are a powerful force within the automotive community. With their passion, knowledge, and influence, they are breaking stereotypes, leading by example, and empowering the next generation of enthusiasts. Whether they are wrenching on engines, burning rubber on the track, or sharing their experiences online, one thing is clear: car girls are here to stay, and they are driving change in the world of automobiles.
